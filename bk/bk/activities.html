Interests

I am interested in Abstract Algebra, Physics, Astronomy, Reading, Music, Traveling, Painting and Sketching etc.

Physics

Boltzmann, bunch of students in IIT Madras, who are interested in Physics meet every Saturday to discuss various topics in Physics. We have a broad theme for every semester and explore the areas that are usually not covered in classrooms. The topic for Aug-Nov 2010 is 'Random Matrix Theory and its applications'.

Astronomy

I am enthusiastic to know more about the beginning of the universe, stellar evolution, solar system, dark matter & energy telescopes and radio astronomy. I also love observing the night sky both through naked eye and through telescope. I am a part of Astronomy Club in IIT Madras. We are a group of students who enjoy learning, exploring and understanding Universe.

Music

I have always enjoyed listening to Music, especially Classical Music. I admire the rhythm and melody in both Carnatic and Hindustani. I love to listen to instrumental classical music, veena, flute and violin in particular. I am learning Carnatic Classical Music Vocal and Veena from Ms. Pavana Acahar for the past 8 months.

Travel

Visiting new places and people always excites me. I like to tour with my family and friends. Recently I visited Nainital and Mussoorie with my family. I have been to Delhi, Agra, Shimla, Manali, Kolkata, Ooty, Kodaikanal and most of the temple towns in South India.

Reading

  • 'Atlas Shrugged' by Ayn Rand
  • 'Swami and His Friends', 'Malgudi Days', 'The Guide' and other short stories by R. K. Narayan.
  • 'Diary of Anne Frank'
  • 'Harry Potter Series' by J. K. Rowling.
