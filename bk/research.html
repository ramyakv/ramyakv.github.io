Research Projects

A Study of Analytical Approximations for the Performance Analysis of IEEE 802.11 Wireless Local Area Networks
  • Summer Research Project: May - July 2010
  • Guide: Prof. Anurag Kumar, Department of Electrical Communciations Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.
  • Application of Mean Field Limit Based Approximations to wireless networks.
    • Analysed the behavior of Scaled Node Attempt Model of a wireless network.
    • Simulated the system and compared the results with the approximate ODE solutions.
    • Analysed the local stability of the equilibrium point and proved the existence of a zero eigen value for a general scaled node attempt model.
  • Analysis of IEEE 802.11n Infrastructure Wireless Local Area Netowrs with Channel Errors
    • Analysed the 802.11 WLAN with channel errors and showed that the sufficient condition for the existence of unique fixed point for an error-less system holds good for the system with channel errors also.
    • Modeled a Single Cell IEEE 802.11n Infrastructure WLAN with two class of nodes, one with good channel conditions and the other with channel errors.
