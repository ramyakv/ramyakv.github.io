About Me

I am a senior year student (Bachelor of Technology) in the Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai, India.

I am broadly interested in Communications and specifically in Coding Theory, Information Theory and Wireless Networks. I am deeply inclined towards Mathematics, Abstract Algebra in particular. The elegance and richness of Mathematics and its wide applications has always fascinated me, which has heavily influenced my academic interests.

I am currently working on the applications of 'Generalized Discrete Fourier Transforms' in OFDM under the guidance of Prof. David Koilpillai for my B.Tech Project. I also worked on 'Analytical Approximations for the Performance Analysis of IEEE 802.11 Wireless Local Area Networks' as a part of my Summer Research Project under Prof. Anurag Kumar during May-July 2010 in Department of Electrical Communication Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.

I was born and brought up in Sagar (Shimoga district) in Karnataka. I did my schooling St. Joseph's Higher Primary School, Nirmala Girls High School and Government Pre-University College in Sagar.
